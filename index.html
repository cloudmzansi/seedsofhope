ABOUT US

Seeds of Hope Queensburgh is a community-based nonprofit organization dedicated to relieving food insecurity in Queensburgh, KZN. We believe that when families have reliable access to nutritious food, they can focus on building better futures for themselves and their children.

OUR MISSION

Our approach is rooted in community partnership and practical solutions. We start with our Dry Meal Packs pilot program and envision expanding to a comprehensive feeding scheme serving schools and our own venue 7 days a week.

Our Vision: To establish a comprehensive feeding scheme operating 7 days a week, serving both local schools and our own dedicated venue.

OUR IMPACT

We're excited to launch our first initiative: the Community Meal Packs program. This community-driven program provides nutritious, shelf-stable meals to families in need right here in Queensburgh.

COMMUNITY MEAL PACKS

Our project has just started, but our vision is clear: to reach as many families as possible with nutritious, easy-to-prepare meal packs. Each pack contains carefully portioned dry goods that are shelf-stable, nutritious, and simple to cook - perfect for families who need reliable access to wholesome meals.

Our Vision: To establish a comprehensive feeding scheme operating 5 days a week, serving both local schools and our own dedicated venue.

OUR MEAL PACK VARIETIES

ENERGY PACK

A complete meal foundation with essential nutrients for energy and sustenance.

  • 1 cup rice or maize meal
  • 1/2 cup dried beans or lentils
  • 1 small sachet of stock (optional)
  • 1 small sachet of sugar or tea/coffee (optional)

BREAKFAST BOOST PACK

Start the day right with a nutritious breakfast that provides lasting energy.

  • 1 cup oats or instant porridge
  • 2 tablespoons powdered milk or soy milk powder
  • Small pack of dried fruit
  • Small sugar/honey sachet (optional)

PROTEIN & SNACK PACK

Protein-rich options with convenient snacks for between meals.

  • 1 tin of baked beans or pilchards (or dried beans as alternative)
  • Portion of hard biscuits/rusks
  • Optional sachet of peanut butter or powdered milk

Nutritious & Balanced

Each pack is carefully designed to provide essential nutrients and balanced nutrition for families.

Shelf-Stable

All ingredients are dry and non-perishable, ensuring they last longer and are safe to store.

Easy to Prepare

Simple cooking instructions included with each pack - no complex recipes or special equipment needed.
