About Us

Seeds of Hope Queensburgh is a community-driven nonprofit dedicated to ensuring no child in our community goes hungry.

Message from Our Founder

"Growing up in Queensburgh, I saw how food insecurity affects families every day. Children went to school hungry, parents struggled to put meals on the table, and families were forced to make impossible choices between food and other necessities.

No child should have to learn on an empty stomach. Every family deserves dignity, and no parent should have to wonder where the next meal will come from.

Seeds of Hope Queensburgh was born from this belief. Food insecurity isn't only about hunger — it's about hope, opportunity, and the chance for families to focus on building better futures. Our first step is a soup kitchen pilot at Hillary Methodist Church, with plans to expand into schools and, ultimately, a dedicated feeding center serving the whole community.

Together, we can break the cycle of hunger and give every child in Queensburgh the nourishment they need to thrive."

— Andrew Michaels, Founder & Executive Director

What We do

We focus on feeding school children, providing nutritious meals at schools and community hubs, and operating soup kitchen outreach with weekly hot meals at Hillary Methodist Church. While children are our priority, we believe no one in need should go hungry.
