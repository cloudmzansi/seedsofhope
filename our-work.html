Our Work

Explore our comprehensive programs and initiatives designed to address food insecurity in Queensburgh.

Our Journey to End Hunger

Follow our roadmap as we build a comprehensive support system for families facing food insecurity in Queensburgh. Each milestone brings us closer to our vision of a hunger-free community.

Foundation Phase

Our pilot soup kitchen at Hillary Methodist Church provides nutritious meals once a week to families in need. We serve hot, balanced meals with the help of dedicated volunteers and community partners.

Community Expansion

We're expanding our network of local churches, schools, and community organizations to identify families most in need of support and maximize our impact in fighting food insecurity.

Vision Realized

Our ultimate vision: establishing our own dedicated feeding center operating 7 days a week for consistent access to nutritious meals throughout Queensburgh. This will be a comprehensive community hub for food security.
